"Stacks" is what is called and their is more than one way of utilizing stacks in your website, but before I go there I will list a couple options stacks provides. Horizontal stacks, vertical stacks, and button stacks.

With stacks you can utilize it on your website as a means of adding items on as a list. It can be a grocery list, chores, items that are required to complete a project, whatever the case. And what stacks does is allows you to order it visually either horizontally,or vertically. As you can see you can also stack buttons on the page as you see with "save changes" and "cancel" (vertically), or maybe an inline form as you see with "add your item here", difinetly have options to play with, options to customize and make neat.
